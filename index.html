There's a version of quiet I didn't know until you — the kind that doesn't feel empty, but full. Full of everything we don't need to say out loud.

I keep thinking about how many versions of a life exist — and how in every one I can imagine, I'd want you in it. Not by coincidence. By choice, again and again.
